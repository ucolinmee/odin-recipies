Chicken Rice

Hainanese chicken rice is a dish of poached chicken and seasoned rice, served with chilli sauce and usually with cucumber garnishes. It was created by immigrants from Hainan in southern China and adapted from the Hainanese dish Wenchang chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Water
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Boil chicken in pot until soft. Remove chicken from pot when done.
  2. Using same water to boil chicken, cook the rice in the chicken, adding slices of ginger and garlic to it as well.
  3. Plate chicken with rice, add soy sauce and chilli sauce to your liking.
